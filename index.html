My name is Andrew Buckingham. I am a husband, world traveler, and aspiring web developer. "What do you do?"

After 20 years of playing around with building simple websites and geeking out in my spare time, I’ve taken the plunge, and decided to learn how to solve real problems, and build real, production-quality web applications. I am currently completing an intensive online training program, through Tealeaf Academy. The program primarily focuses on Ruby and Rails development.

For the past few years, I’ve been a Blackboard Administrator at a local university. But what I really want to do is build web applications that improve the quality of life of people around the world. I decided it was time to take charge of my career, and do something that is both meaningful and fun. I’m so grateful for this opportunity, and I’m excited by the possibilities!
